
Vector:
	
	- It is almost similar to ArrayList.
	- It is Synchronized.
	- It maintains the insertion order.
	- Vector allows duplicate values.
	
Methods in Vectors:

	- addElement(value): This will add the element to the vector.
	
	- capacity(): This will print the capacity of the Vector. The default capacity of the vector is 10. Whenever the size goes beyond 10, the capacity is doubled.
