
Association:
	
	- It is relationship between two separate classes through their objects.
	
	- Example, 
		- One to One
		- One To Many
		- One to Many
		
- A Relation can be represented as logical links between two or more entities or objects.

- Classification of Relations:
	- Association
	- Aggregation
	- Composition

Association:

	- Relation resulted by the cooperation between classes.
	
	- Communication between the objects of the classes
	
	- Object in a relation can have both dependent and independent lifecycles.
	
Cardinality:
	
	- Refers to the number of cardinal (basic) members in a set.
	- One to One
	- One to Many
	- Many to Many
	
- Association joins two entirely different entities.
